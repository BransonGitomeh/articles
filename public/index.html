I Want to Fall In Love with You - come to me on a journey.

A wonderful naration from miss Gaturi.

Gaturi Wanjiku 5 Aug 2019

I want to fall in love with you. Maybe I’m not there right now. In point of fact, I am not there now because I haven’t met you yet. I want to let you know In advance that I have difficulties construing my feelings sometimes, when everything I feel is so resolute. I want to be sure it’s love, before I make any promises. But I can promise you this: I want to fall in love with you because you will be the one I have been waiting for and the one who was meant for me.

I want falling in love with you, and loving you to be the feeling that will make me understand life, past heartbreaks, the crushes I didn’t act upon…. up until the moment that I will meet you.

I pray, that it is a promise I will make in my heart. Noiseless, Vocal, Indestructible either by distance, space and time. I hope it is the feeling of knowing that I will totally and absolutely matter to someone else other than my parents and my sisters. I hope we shall have the courage to show it to each other, and to act it out, loving out loud.

I want it to be about feeling the callouses on your hands, or the hairs around your ears, or the curve where your shoulder and neck meet, being face to cheek. I also want it to be colossally about the broadness of your chest, where my head will eventually have a permanent place right next to your heart.

I hope it is about the firmness of your thighs and the curviness of your muscles. I want it to be about clinging on your built forearms and feeling the weight of your body on and against mine. I want it to be about your breath on my neck, the softness of my lips against yours. I hope I use that moment to discover the places that make you laugh and the ones that make you moan. I hope it will be about trust, and defenselessness, because I know that sleeping next to someone not with someone renders us completely susceptible and unguarded. I want that when our hard exteriors fall away in slumber, when we are soft and supple and childlike will be next to each other.
